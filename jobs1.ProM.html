Job Overview

Lead product development from ideation to launch. Work with cross-functional teams, define product roadmaps, and deliver exceptional user experiences that drive business growth. As a Product Manager at Microsoft, you'll have the opportunity to work on products used by millions of people worldwide, making a real impact on how people work and collaborate.

Key Responsibilities

  • Define and execute product vision, strategy, and roadmap aligned with business objectives
  • Lead product development from conception through launch and iteration
  • Work closely with engineering, design, marketing, and sales teams to deliver exceptional products
  • Conduct market research, competitive analysis, and customer interviews to identify opportunities
  • Define product requirements, user stories, and acceptance criteria
  • Prioritize features and manage product backlog using Agile methodologies
  • Analyze product metrics and user feedback to drive continuous improvement
  • Present product updates and roadmaps to stakeholders and leadership
  • Make data-driven decisions using analytics and A/B testing
  • Collaborate with UX/UI designers to create intuitive user experiences
  • Manage product launches, go-to-market strategies, and customer adoption
  • Monitor product performance and KPIs, making adjustments as needed
  • Build and maintain relationships with key stakeholders and customers
  • Stay updated with industry trends, emerging technologies, and best practices

Required Qualifications

Professional Experience:

  • 4-7 years of product management experience in technology companies
  • Proven track record of successfully launching and scaling products
  • Experience working with cross-functional teams in Agile environments
  • Strong understanding of software development lifecycle and processes
  • Experience with product analytics tools (Google Analytics, Mixpanel, Amplitude, etc.)
  • Demonstrated ability to use data and metrics to drive product decisions
  • Experience conducting user research and translating insights into product features
  • Background in SaaS, cloud, or enterprise software is highly preferred

Technical Skills:

  • Strong analytical skills with ability to interpret complex data sets
  • Proficiency in product management tools (JIRA, Confluence, Azure DevOps, etc.)
  • Understanding of APIs, databases, and modern software architectures
  • Experience with A/B testing frameworks and statistical analysis
  • Familiarity with design tools (Figma, Adobe XD) for collaborating with designers
  • Knowledge of SQL for data analysis is a plus

Soft Skills:

  • Excellent communication and presentation skills
  • Strong leadership abilities with experience influencing without authority
  • Strategic thinking with attention to execution details
  • Customer-centric mindset with empathy for user needs
  • Problem-solving skills and ability to navigate ambiguity
  • Stakeholder management and negotiation skills
  • Self-motivated and able to work independently in remote environment
  • Adaptability and resilience in fast-paced environments

Preferred Qualifications

  • MBA or Master's degree in Business, Computer Science, or related field
  • Experience with Microsoft products and ecosystem
  • Background in B2B enterprise software or cloud services
  • Experience managing products with global user bases
  • Track record of working with distributed remote teams
  • Technical background or engineering degree
  • Experience with product-led growth strategies
  • Previous experience in product marketing or business development
  • Certifications in Agile, Scrum, or Product Management

Application Process

To apply for this position, please complete the application form and upload your resume. Our recruitment team will review applications on a rolling basis.

The hiring process typically includes:

  • Initial screening call with HR team (30 minutes)
  • Product sense interview (60 minutes) - Discuss product thinking and problem-solving
  • Analytical interview (60 minutes) - Case study and data analysis
  • Technical interview (60 minutes) - Understanding of technology and systems
  • Leadership and behavioral interview (60 minutes)
  • Final round with hiring manager and senior leadership

The entire process usually takes 3-4 weeks. We value your time and will keep you informed at every stage.
