Frequently Asked Questions

Quick answers to common questions about EasyJobs

How do I create an account?

Click on the 'Register' button in the top right corner, fill in your details, and you'll be ready to start your job search or post openings.

Is EasyJobs free for job seekers?

Yes! Job seekers can create profiles, search jobs, and apply to unlimited positions completely free of charge.

How can employers post jobs?

Employers can register for a business account and choose from our flexible pricing plans to start posting job listings.

What's your response time?

We typically respond to all inquiries within 24 hours during business days. Urgent matters are prioritized.

Can I edit my profile after creating it?

Absolutely! You can update your profile, resume, and preferences anytime from your account dashboard.

Do you offer customer support?

Yes! Our dedicated support team is available via email, phone, and live chat to assist you with any questions.
