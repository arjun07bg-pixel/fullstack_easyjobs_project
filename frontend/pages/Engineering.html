Engineering Career Milestones

Graduate Level (0-2 Years)

Project Level (3-7 Years)

Project Engineer or Design Lead. Leading specific projects or manufacturing units independently. ₹10 – 22 LPA.

Management Level (8–15 Years)

Project Manager or Principal Consultant. Strategic planning for large-scale infrastructure. ₹30 – 55 LPA.

Leadership Level (15+ Years)

VP of Engineering or Operations Director. Driving enterprise-wide growth and industrial standards. ₹70 – 120+ LPA.
