Finance Career Progression

Entry Level (0-3 Years)

Accounts Executive or Finance Trainee. Focus on transaction processing and basic reconciliations. ₹3–6 LPA.

Mid Level (4-8 Years)

Finance Manager or Senior Auditor. Responsibility for regional budgets or departmental audits. ₹10–18 LPA.

Senior Level (8–15 Years)

Director of Finance or VP - Banking. Strategic decision making and multi-national portfolio management. ₹25–50 LPA.

Expert Level (15+ Years)

Chief Financial Officer (CFO) or Managing Director. Driving global financial strategy and organizational value. ₹60–150+ LPA.
