Job Overview

Create beautiful and intuitive user experiences for our e-commerce platform. Conduct user research, design wireframes and prototypes, and collaborate with developers to bring designs to life. Join Flipkart's design team to create experiences that millions of users love and trust.

Key Responsibilities

  • Design intuitive and visually appealing user interfaces for web and mobile applications
  • Conduct user research through interviews, surveys, and usability testing to understand user needs
  • Create user personas, user journeys, and information architecture diagrams
  • Design wireframes, mockups, and interactive prototypes using Figma and Adobe XD
  • Collaborate closely with product managers, developers, and stakeholders throughout the design process
  • Develop and maintain design systems and component libraries for consistency
  • Present design concepts and rationale to stakeholders and incorporate feedback
  • Conduct A/B testing and analyze user behavior data to optimize designs
  • Ensure designs are accessible and follow WCAG guidelines
  • Stay updated with latest design trends, tools, and best practices in UX/UI
  • Iterate on designs based on user feedback and data-driven insights
  • Participate in design critiques and provide constructive feedback to team members

Required Qualifications

Design Skills:

  • 2-4 years of professional experience in UX/UI design
  • Expert proficiency in Figma and Adobe XD for design and prototyping
  • Strong understanding of user-centered design principles and methodologies
  • Experience conducting user research and usability testing
  • Ability to create wireframes, user flows, and interactive prototypes
  • Knowledge of design systems and component-based design approaches
  • Understanding of responsive design and mobile-first principles
  • Familiarity with HTML, CSS, and basic frontend concepts
  • Strong visual design skills including typography, color theory, and layout
  • Experience with design collaboration tools like Zeplin or InVision

Soft Skills:

  • Excellent communication and presentation skills
  • Strong problem-solving and analytical thinking abilities
  • Ability to empathize with users and advocate for their needs
  • Collaborative mindset and ability to work in cross-functional teams
  • Attention to detail and commitment to pixel-perfect designs
  • Self-motivated with ability to manage multiple projects simultaneously
  • Openness to feedback and willingness to iterate on designs

Preferred Qualifications

  • Bachelor's or Master's degree in Design, HCI, or related field
  • Experience designing for e-commerce or marketplace platforms
  • Knowledge of accessibility standards and inclusive design practices
  • Familiarity with animation and micro-interaction design
  • Experience with design thinking workshops and ideation sessions
  • Understanding of Agile development methodologies
  • Experience mentoring junior designers
  • Knowledge of user analytics tools like Google Analytics or Mixpanel
  • Portfolio demonstrating strong UX process and visual design skills

About Flipkart

Flipkart is India's leading e-commerce marketplace with a registered customer base of over 450 million. We are committed to transforming commerce in India through technology. Our platform offers over 150 million products across 80+ categories, providing customers with a delightful shopping experience.

At Flipkart, design is at the heart of everything we do. Our design team works on solving complex problems at scale, creating intuitive experiences that make online shopping simple, enjoyable, and accessible to all Indians. Join us to be part of a team that's redefining e-commerce design in India.

Work Culture

We foster a culture of innovation, creativity, and collaboration. Our hybrid work model allows you to work from our Mumbai office 3 days a week and from home for the rest. The office is equipped with state-of-the-art design facilities, collaboration spaces, and amenities to support your best work.

We believe in continuous learning and provide opportunities for professional growth through workshops, design conferences, and mentorship programs. Regular design critique sessions and team activities help foster a supportive and creative environment.

Application Process

To apply for this position, please complete the application form and upload your resume. IMPORTANT: Include a link to your online portfolio showcasing your UX/UI design work. Applications without portfolios will not be reviewed.

Our hiring process typically includes:

  • Portfolio review by our design team
  • Initial screening call with HR (30 minutes)
  • Design challenge or case study presentation (take-home assignment)
  • Design interview with senior designers (90 minutes)
  • Final round with design head and product team

The entire process usually takes 2-3 weeks. We'll keep you updated at every stage and provide feedback regardless of the outcome.

Portfolio Requirements

Your portfolio should demonstrate:

  • Your UX process from research to final designs
  • Case studies showing problem-solving approach
  • Visual design skills and attention to detail
  • Ability to design for different platforms (web and mobile)
  • Examples of user research and testing
  • Wireframes, prototypes, and final designs
