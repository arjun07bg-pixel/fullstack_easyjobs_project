Engineering Career Ladder

Entry Level (0-3 Years)

Graduate Engineer Trainee (GET) or Junior Engineer. Focus on learning site/design processes and assisting seniors. ₹3–6 LPA.

Mid Level (4-8 Years)

Assistant Manager or Design Lead. Taking ownership of specific modules/sites and small team management. ₹8–16 LPA.

Senior Level (8–15 Years)

Chief Engineer or Project Director. Overseeing entire project lifecycles and high-level strategy. ₹20–45 LPA.

Expert Level (15+ Years)

VP - Operations or Head of Engineering. Leading national business units and driving organizational innovation. ₹50–120+ LPA.
