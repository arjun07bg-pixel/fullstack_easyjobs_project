Sales & Marketing Career Path

Entry Level (0-2 Years)

Marketing Coordinator or Sales Associate. Learn market Research and customer interaction fundamentals. ₹3–6 LPA.

Mid Level (3-6 Years)

Brand Manager or Sales Executive. Handle specific territories or product lines independently. ₹7–14 LPA.

Senior Level (7–12 Years)

Marketing Director or Regional Sales Manager. Setting strategic goals and leading departments. ₹18–35 LPA.

Expert Level (12+ Years)

Chief Marketing Officer (CMO) or VP of Sales. Driving enterprise-wide growth and brand identity. ₹40–100+ LPA.
