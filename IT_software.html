IT Software Career Path

Entry Level (0-2 Years)

Junior Developer or Intern. Learn version control, basic coding, and testing. **₹3–5 LPA.**

Mid Level (2-5 Years)

Full Stack Developer or Software Engineer. Handle modules and design scalable systems. **₹6–10 LPA.**

Senior Level (5–10 Years)

Senior Developer or Tech Lead. Lead projects and mentor juniors. **₹10–18 LPA.**

Expert Level (10+ Years)

Architect or Engineering Manager. Oversee architecture & technology strategy. **₹20–35+ LPA.**
