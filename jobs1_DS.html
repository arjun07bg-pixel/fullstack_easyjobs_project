Job Overview

Join our data science team to work on cutting-edge ML projects. You'll analyze large datasets, build predictive models, and help drive business decisions through data-driven insights. This role offers an exciting opportunity to work with state-of-the-art technologies and collaborate with world-class data scientists at Amazon.

Key Responsibilities

  • Analyze large and complex datasets to extract meaningful insights and patterns
  • Design, develop, and deploy machine learning models to solve business problems
  • Build predictive models using advanced statistical and ML techniques
  • Collaborate with cross-functional teams including product, engineering, and business stakeholders
  • Create data visualizations and dashboards to communicate findings effectively
  • Perform A/B testing and experimentation to validate hypotheses
  • Optimize existing models for better performance and accuracy
  • Develop automated data pipelines and ETL processes
  • Stay updated with latest developments in data science and ML technologies
  • Mentor junior data scientists and contribute to team knowledge sharing

Required Qualifications

Technical Skills:

  • 3-5 years of professional experience in data science or related field
  • Strong proficiency in Python and data science libraries (NumPy, Pandas, Scikit-learn)
  • Solid understanding of machine learning algorithms (supervised and unsupervised)
  • Experience with deep learning frameworks like TensorFlow or PyTorch
  • Proficiency in SQL and experience with relational databases
  • Strong foundation in statistics, probability, and mathematical modeling
  • Experience with data visualization tools (Matplotlib, Seaborn, Tableau)
  • Knowledge of big data technologies (Hadoop, Spark) is a plus
  • Familiarity with cloud platforms (AWS, Azure, or GCP)
  • Experience with version control systems (Git)

Soft Skills:

  • Excellent analytical and problem-solving abilities
  • Strong communication skills to explain complex concepts to non-technical stakeholders
  • Ability to work independently and in team environments
  • Detail-oriented with focus on delivering high-quality results
  • Curiosity and passion for continuous learning

Preferred Qualifications

  • Master's or PhD in Computer Science, Statistics, Mathematics, or related field
  • Experience with natural language processing (NLP) or computer vision
  • Knowledge of recommendation systems and personalization algorithms
  • Publications in top-tier conferences or journals
  • Experience with MLOps and model deployment in production
  • Familiarity with agile development methodologies
  • Contributions to open-source data science projects
  • Experience in e-commerce or retail analytics

  • Performance-based bonuses and stock options (RSUs)

  • Flexible working hours and work-life balance

  • Employee discount on Amazon products and services
  • Parental leave and family support programs

About Amazon

Amazon is guided by four principles: customer obsession rather than competitor focus, passion for invention, commitment to operational excellence, and long-term thinking. We are driven by the excitement of building technologies, inventing products, and providing services that change lives.

Our data science team plays a crucial role in Amazon's success by leveraging data to improve customer experiences, optimize operations, and drive innovation across all business units. Join us to work on problems at unprecedented scale and make a real impact.

Work Environment

This is an office-based position located in our modern Hyderabad facility. You'll work in a collaborative environment with state-of-the-art infrastructure, including dedicated data science labs, high-performance computing resources, and meeting spaces designed for innovation.

Application Process

To apply for this position, please complete the application form and upload your resume along with a cover letter highlighting your relevant data science projects. Our recruitment team will review applications on a rolling basis and contact qualified candidates within 5-7 business days.

  • - Case study presentation
  • - Behavioral interviews

Growth and Development
